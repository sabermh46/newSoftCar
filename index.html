Tired of losing track of your rental cars? Looking for an easy- -to-use app to manage your rental service?

SoftCar is an all-in-one app that enables you to easily & efficiently manage your rental service.

Softcar makes it easy for your customers to rent a car, find its location, & return it, all through the softcar app.

Its seamless and user-friendly interface makes it the best option for your rental service.

Keep track of reserved & available cars
Have records of payment information
Identify who damaged the car
See statistics of reservations

Keep track of everything with the dashboard display

The dashboard displays all the information you need to easily keep track of your vehicles' location, upcoming reservations, available cars, payment details and damage reports.

With Softcar, your customers will easily reserve a car right from their phone

Your customers can access the calender & choose a date and time to rent a car, and pay directly from the app.
